About this jar

What it is.

The sauce line in a shaker. Fire Dust takes the same NoodleBomb flavor idea — bold, savory, built for everyday food — and turns it into a dry blend you can shake on at the table. Korean chili (gochugaru) brings warm red heat, toasted sesame and roasted garlic add aroma and crunch, and shiitake powder lays down deep umami underneath. Pour a sauce for the glossy hit, then finish with a shake of Fire Dust for texture and heat.

Shake lightly before use. Start with a little, taste, and add more for extra heat and crunch. Keep the cap closed and store cool and dry so the chili stays bright. Built for ramen, eggs, rice, wings, fries, popcorn, grilled chicken, roasted vegetables, and dumplings.
