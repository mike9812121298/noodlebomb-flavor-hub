Quick answer

Fire Dust is the shake-on ramen spice bomb.

If you searched for a ramen spice bomb, you probably want a fast way to make packet noodles taste bigger without building a full recipe. Fire Dust is built for that job: Korean chili for warm heat, garlic for punch, sesame for round savory flavor, shiitake for depth, and visible crunch for the final hit.

Fire Dust is not one of the pour-on sauces. It is a 3.2 oz dry blend jar at $11.99. Use it when the bowl needs a shake. Use the $29.99 Ramen Sauce Trio when you want Original, Spicy Tokyo, and Citrus Shoyu in 7 fl oz bottles.

Fast uses

Where Fire Dust hits hardest.

Instant ramen

Cook the noodles, build the bowl, then shake Fire Dust at the end so the flakes and sesame stay loud.

Wings and fries

Shake while the food is hot so the garlic, chili, and sesame grab the surface fast.

Eggs and rice

Use Fire Dust when plain eggs or rice need chili heat, garlic aroma, and a little crunch.
