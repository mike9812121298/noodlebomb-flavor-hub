Original · the all-purpose bottle

Umami recipes.

10 MINRAMEN

Original Weeknight Ramen Bowl

Turn a plain pack into a real bowl. Original brings deep soy, garlic, ginger, sesame, and balanced umami.

Ingredients: 1 serving ramen noodles, 3-4 tbsp NoodleBomb, soft egg, scallions, corn, sesame.

  1. Cook noodles until springy, then drain or move into a hot bowl with a little broth.
  2. Add Original and toss until the noodles are glossy.
  3. Add toppings while everything is still hot.
  4. Finish with Original so the deep soy, garlic, ginger, sesame, and balanced umami comes through.
