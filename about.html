Small batch BOLD FLAVOR. EVERY BITE.
Bonney Lake, WA

Started at dinner. Built for everyday food.

Ashley was trying to fix a simple problem: the ramen bowl needed more than a packet. She wanted something built for the bowl, but useful beyond it.

The first version came together in the kitchen, then moved from family dinners to early testers. Original, Spicy Tokyo, and Citrus Shoyu became the core lineup: savory, bold, and easy to reach for on a weeknight.

Mike handles the operator side. The brand is independent, family-owned, and focused on one job: making everyday food taste better without making dinner complicated.
