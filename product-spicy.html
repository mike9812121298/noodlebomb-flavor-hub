    About this bottle

    What it is.

    The bold one. Heat layered over depth, with roasted chili built on the same garlic-sesame backbone as Original. The slow-burn finish works on wings, fried rice, noodles, and grilled meat. Medium-high on the heat scale, flavor first, steady finish. Built for spice fans who still want the food to taste like food.

    The drizzle cap is built for control: a thin stream for finishing a bowl, a steady pour for marinades and stir-fry. Store unopened in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening for peak flavor. Don’t leave it on the stovetop — heat dulls the top notes.
