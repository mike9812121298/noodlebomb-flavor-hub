    About this bottle

    What it is.

    The bold one. Heat layered over depth — not hot for hot’s sake. Dark soy and roasted chili built on the same garlic-sesame backbone as Original, with a slow-burn finish that stays on your tongue without burying the broth. Medium-high on the heat scale, flavor-forward, never pain-forward. The bottle most heads keep next to the kettle.

    A 7 fl oz bottle is roughly 14 servings — most people get 3 to 4 weeks of regular use. The drizzle cap is built for control: a thin stream for finishing a bowl, a steady pour for marinades and stir-fry. Refrigerate after opening and use within 6 months for peak flavor. Don’t leave it on the stovetop — heat dulls the top notes.
