Good to know

Questions, answered.

How do I earn points?+

You earn 5 points for every $1 on every order. You also get 250 points for joining, 100 for sharing on Facebook, and 250 on your birthday.

What are my points worth?+

100 points = $1 off. Redeem from 100 points up — so 500 points is $5 off, 1,000 is $10, and so on.

How do I redeem?+

Open the Rewards panel (the tab at the corner of any page, or the button below), choose how many points to spend, and you'll get a discount code. Apply it at checkout — that simple.

Do my points expire?+

No. Your points stay in your account until you're ready to use them.

Is it free, and how do I join?+

Completely free. Hit Join NoodleBomb Rewards, sign up with your email, and your 250 welcome points are waiting. Earn on every order you place with that email.
