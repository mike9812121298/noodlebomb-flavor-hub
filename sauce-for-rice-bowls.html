Bowl formula

Rice. Protein. Crunch. Sauce.

Start with warm rice, add one cooked protein, one vegetable, one crunchy topping, and one spoon of NoodleBomb. That is enough to make leftovers taste intentional.

For most bowls, start with 1 tablespoon of sauce. Add another half spoon if the bowl has a lot of rice, chicken, tofu, or roasted vegetables. Finish with scallions, sesame, lime, cucumber, or fried shallots. Tofu guide →

Quick recipe

Crispy rice salmon bowl.

Brush 2 salmon fillets with Original and rest 10 minutes. Press 2 cups cooked short-grain rice flat in a non-stick pan with 2 tablespoons oil; crisp 6 to 8 minutes undisturbed. Sear salmon skin-side down 4 minutes, add another spoon of Original, then finish 2 minutes.

Plate the rice browned-side up, top with salmon, avocado, scallions, sesame, and lime. Add a final spoon of sauce right before eating.
