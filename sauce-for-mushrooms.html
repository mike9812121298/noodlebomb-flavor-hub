Sauce for mushrooms.

Mushrooms already bring savory depth. NoodleBomb Original adds roasted garlic, toasted sesame, and smooth soy so mushroom noodles, rice bowls, eggs, tofu, and steak taste finished fast.

Fast formula

Brown first. Sauce last.

1. Dry the panCook mushrooms until the water cooks off and the edges start to brown.
2. Add fatUse butter, oil, or sesame oil once the mushrooms start taking color.
3. Sauce near the endAdd NoodleBomb after browning so it glazes instead of scorching.
4. Finish with textureScallions, sesame, crispy garlic, herbs, eggs, rice, or noodles make it a meal.

FAQ

Mushroom questions.

Can I use NoodleBomb as sauce for mushrooms?

Yes. Original works especially well with mushrooms because roasted garlic, toasted sesame, and smooth soy build on the natural savory flavor of mushrooms.

Which flavor works best?

Original is the best first pick for mushrooms. Spicy Tokyo works when you want chili heat, and Citrus Shoyu works when the dish needs a brighter finish.

When should I add the sauce?

Brown the mushrooms first, then add NoodleBomb near the end so the sauce coats the mushrooms without burning in the pan.
