Recipe · Garlic bomb ramen

Garlic bomb
chicken ramen.

A fast, loud bowl: hot noodles, shredded chicken, NoodleBomb Original, scallion, sesame, and a soft egg.

Get Original - $11.99 Try the Trio - $29.99 More recipes
10 minutes · 1 bowl

The move is simple: build the broth around Original.

NoodleBomb Original is the everyday garlic-sesame bottle. For this bowl, stir it into hot broth first so the sauce becomes the base instead of sitting on top. Add noodles, cooked chicken, and a few toppings, and dinner stops tasting like a backup plan.

STEP 1NOODLES

Cook the noodles

Use instant ramen, fresh ramen, or whatever noodles are already in the pantry. Cook, drain if needed, and keep them hot.

STEP 2SAUCE

Bomb the broth

Stir 1½ tbsp NoodleBomb Original into 1½ cups hot chicken broth or hot water. Taste once. Add another small drizzle if the bowl wants more punch.

STEP 3CHICKEN

Add chicken

Fold in cooked shredded chicken. Rotisserie chicken works. Leftover grilled chicken works. Keep it easy.

STEP 4TOP

Finish loud

Add soft-boiled egg, scallion, toasted sesame, and chili crisp if you want heat. Serve immediately while the broth is hot.

Ingredients

What you need.

Base

1 pack ramen noodles, 1½ cups hot chicken broth or hot water, and 1½ tbsp NoodleBomb Original.

Protein

½ cup cooked shredded chicken. Rotisserie chicken is perfect when you want this bowl fast.

Toppings

Soft-boiled egg, sliced scallion, toasted sesame seeds, and optional chili crisp or extra Original.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Which NoodleBomb sauce works best for garlic bomb chicken ramen?+

Use Original when you want garlic-sesame depth without extra heat. Use Spicy Tokyo if you want the same bowl hotter.

Can I use rotisserie chicken?+

Yes. Rotisserie chicken is the fastest path. Add it at the end so it warms through without drying out.

Can I make this with instant ramen?+

Yes. Cook the noodles, then use NoodleBomb Original as the main flavor move. Add hot broth or hot water, chicken, and toppings.
